# Sales Analysis with Tableau

## Overview

This project analyzes sales and profit data using Tableau to identify business trends, evaluate performance, and support data-driven decision making.

The analysis focuses on sales growth, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance through interactive dashboards and business reporting.

## Objectives

* Analyze sales performance
* Evaluate profit trends
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Compare regional performance
* Create interactive business dashboards
* Generate actionable business insights

## Tools & Technologies

* Tableau
* Data Visualization
* Business Analytics
* KPI Analysis
* Dashboard Development

## Key Business Questions

* Which products generate the highest sales?
* Which categories are the most profitable?
* How do sales and profit vary across regions?

* Which business segments contribute the most revenue?

## Key Insights

### Sales Performance

The dashboard highlights revenue trends and identifies periods of business growth and decline.

### Profitability Analysis

Profit metrics help identify the most profitable products, categories, and business segments.

### Regional Performance

Regional comparisons reveal geographic differences in sales and profit generation.

### Business Trends

Historical analysis allows tracking long-term business performance and identifying growth opportunities.
